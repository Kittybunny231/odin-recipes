Lemonade Recipe

Description

When life gives you lemons, make a nice cool drink

Ingredients

  • like 6-8 lemons or 1 cup
  • 1 cup of suger
  • 6 cups of water

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup water and 1 cup sugar in a small saucepan. Place over medium-low heat and stir until sugar is dissolved. Do not allow to boil
  2. Cut lemon in half and squeeze lemon juice use spoon to get juice out, strain if needed
  3. Add suger water, lemon juice, and 5 cups of water into pitcher then cool in fridge
