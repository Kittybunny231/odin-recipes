Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Left-over Rice
  • Vegatables like Peas, Carrots, other Green Things
  • Egg or Shrimp
  • Garlic Spice
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Spead enough oil onto pan that it covers half of it, turn on high heat
  2. Put the ingredents that take the longest to cook on first
  3. Add rice and then eggs, make sure the eggs are seprate and let them cook a bit
  4. Add the spices and soy sauce then mix it all together
  5. Remove from heat once everthing is cooked, should take 5-10 min
